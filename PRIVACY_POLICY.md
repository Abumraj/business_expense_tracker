# Privacy Policy - Business Expense Tracker

**Last Updated:** April 2, 2026  
**Effective Date:** April 2, 2026  

Purplegate ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Business Expense Tracker mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Business Data**: Expense records, income data, customer information, invoices, and other financial data you enter into the App
- **User Account**: If you create an account, we collect your email address and basic profile information

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the App, features used, and time spent
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Performance Data**: Crash reports and performance metrics

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect your location data
- We do not access your contacts or photos without explicit permission
- We do not collect sensitive personal information beyond what's necessary for the App's functionality

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- **Provide Services**: Deliver the core functionality of the Business Expense Tracker
- **Improve the App**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
- **Customer Support**: Respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
- **Legal Compliance**: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage
- **Primary Storage**: All your business data is stored locally on your device
- **Control**: You have complete control over your data
- **Privacy**: No business data is transmitted to our servers without your explicit consent

### 3.2 Security Measures
- **Encryption**: Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
- **Access Control**: Strict access controls prevent unauthorized access
- **Regular Updates**: We regularly update our security practices

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: With trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the App
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
- **Business Transfer**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request access to your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal data
- **Export**: Export your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out**: Disable certain data collection features

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the country where our servers are located, your information may be transferred, stored, and processed in a country other than your own.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- **Email**: privacy@purplegate.bet
- **App Support**: Through the App's support feature

## 10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide the services and for legitimate business purposes, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

## 11. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of the sale of personal information.
